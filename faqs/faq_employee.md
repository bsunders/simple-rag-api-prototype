# TechFlow Solutions Employee Handbook

## What is our unlimited PTO policy?
TechFlow offers unlimited PTO with a minimum requirement of 15 days per year. Employees must get approval for time off exceeding 2 consecutive weeks. PTO requests require 2-week notice except for emergencies.

## How does our equity vesting schedule work?
New hires receive equity grants vesting over 5 years: 10% after year 1, then 22.5% each subsequent year. Equity grants are reviewed annually in March with potential for additional grants based on performance.

## What are our remote work guidelines?
Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM Pacific Time when all team members should be available. Monthly in-person team meetings are required for all remote employees.

## How does our healthcare plan work?
TechFlow covers 100% of employee health insurance premiums and 80% for family members. We offer medical, dental, and vision coverage through Aetna. Open enrollment is in November with coverage starting January 1st.

## What is our professional development budget?
Each employee receives $3,000 annually for professional development including conferences, courses, certifications, and books. Unused budget doesn't roll over to the next year. Submit receipts to HR for reimbursement.

## What are our office hours and holidays?
Standard office hours are 9 AM to 6 PM Monday through Friday. We observe all major US holidays plus a company-wide winter break from December 24th to January 2nd. Juneteenth and Indigenous Peoples Day are also company holidays.

## How does our 401k matching work?
TechFlow matches 100% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary. Matching is immediate with no vesting period. We use Fidelity as our 401k provider with low-cost index fund options available.
