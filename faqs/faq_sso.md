# SSO FAQ

Q: How do I enable SSO?
A: Contact your admin to enable SSO for your account.

Q: Which SSO providers does TechFlow support?
A: We support Google Workspace, Microsoft Azure AD, Okta, and Salesforce as SSO providers. Contact IT to configure your preferred provider.

Q: What happens if SSO is down?
A: If SSO is unavailable, you can use the "Login with Password" option on the login page as a backup authentication method.

Q: Can I use SSO on mobile devices?
A: Yes, SSO works on mobile devices through our mobile app. Download the TechFlow app from the App Store or Google Play and use "Login with SSO".

Q: How do I troubleshoot SSO login issues?
A: First, clear your browser cache and cookies. If issues persist, check with your SSO provider (Google, Microsoft, etc.) and ensure you're signed in there. Contact IT if problems continue.

Q: Is SSO required for all employees?
A: SSO is strongly recommended but not mandatory. However, all employees in Finance, Legal, and Executive teams are required to use SSO for compliance reasons.
