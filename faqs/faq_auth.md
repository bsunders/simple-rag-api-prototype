# Auth FAQ

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use the reset link on the login page.

Q: What are the password requirements?
A: Passwords must be at least 12 characters long, include uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days.

Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?
A: Go to Account Settings > Security and click "Enable 2FA". Scan the QR code with your authenticator app and enter the verification code.

Q: What should I do if my account is locked?
A: Account lockouts occur after 5 failed login attempts. Wait 15 minutes or contact IT support at support@techflow.com to unlock your account immediately.

Q: Can I use my personal email for login?
A: No, you must use your company email address ending in @techflow.com for all authentication purposes.

Q: How long do login sessions last?
A: Active sessions expire after 8 hours of inactivity. You'll need to re-authenticate after this period for security reasons.
