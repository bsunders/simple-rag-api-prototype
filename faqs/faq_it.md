# IT Support FAQ

Q: How do I get help with technical issues?
A: Submit a ticket through our ServiceNow portal at help.techflow.com or email support@techflow.com. For urgent issues, call the IT helpdesk at (555) 123-4567.

Q: What software can I install on my work laptop?
A: You can install approved software from our Software Center. For new software requests, submit an IT ticket with business justification. Personal software installation requires IT approval.

Q: How do I connect to the company VPN?
A: Download Cisco AnyConnect from the Software Center. Use your company credentials with server address vpn.techflow.com. Enable auto-connect for seamless remote access.

Q: What are our data backup policies?
A: All work files must be stored in OneDrive or company servers. Local files are not automatically backed up. Critical data is backed up nightly with 30-day retention.

Q: How do I report a security incident?
A: Immediately contact the security team at security@techflow.com or call the security hotline at (555) 911-HELP. Do not forward suspicious emails or click unknown links.

Q: What are our email retention policies?
A: Emails are retained for 7 years for compliance. Deleted emails can be recovered for 30 days. Use retention tags for important emails requiring longer preservation.

Q: Can I use personal devices for work?
A: Personal devices must be registered through our MDM system and meet security requirements. Install the Company Portal app and enroll your device with IT approval.
