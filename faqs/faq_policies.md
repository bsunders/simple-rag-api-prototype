# Company Policies FAQ

## What is our expense reimbursement policy?
Employees can expense reasonable business costs including travel, meals, and supplies. Submit expenses through Concur within 30 days with receipts. Meals are limited to $50 per day for domestic travel and $75 for international.

## How does our code of conduct work?
All employees must complete annual ethics training and sign the code of conduct. Report violations to ethics@techflow.com or use the anonymous hotline. We have zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or retaliation.

## What is our social media policy?
Employees may not speak on behalf of TechFlow on personal social media. Use disclaimer "views are my own" when discussing work-related topics. Confidential information must never be shared publicly.

## How do performance reviews work?
Reviews occur quarterly with formal annual reviews in December. Employees set goals with managers and receive feedback on technical skills, collaboration, and company values. Promotion discussions happen during annual reviews.

## What is our diversity and inclusion commitment?
TechFlow is committed to building diverse teams and inclusive culture. We have employee resource groups, unconscious bias training, and diverse hiring practices. The Chief Diversity Officer reports quarterly to the board.

## How does our referral bonus program work?
Employees receive $2,000 for successful referrals who stay 6 months ($1,000) and 12 months ($1,000). Referrals must be submitted before the candidate applies. Bonuses are paid with regular payroll and subject to taxes.
